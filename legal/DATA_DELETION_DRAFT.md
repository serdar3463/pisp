# PISP Data Deletion Draft

PISP is local-first. Users can erase local data from the Vault screen with "Erase local vault and restart".

For future server-side data, PISP should provide:

- A public deletion request form.
- Support email or support portal.
- Identity verification for deletion requests.
- A deletion SLA, for example 30 days.
- Confirmation when deletion is complete.

Draft deletion URL:

https://pisp.example/delete-data
