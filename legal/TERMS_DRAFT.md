# PISP Terms of Service Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-15

This draft must be reviewed by legal counsel before public release.

## Service

PISP provides a local-first personal information vault, consent control interface, QR-based information exchange and proof ledger features.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the accuracy of information they enter and for reviewing disclosure requests before approving sharing.

## No Professional Advice

PISP does not provide legal, medical, financial or identity verification advice. Any disclosure decision remains the user's responsibility unless a regulated production service agreement says otherwise.

## Sensitive Data

Users should take extra care before entering or sharing special category data such as health, biometric, political, religious or financial information.

## Availability

The service may change as the product develops. Beta versions may contain limitations.
